Do you want to support my journey?

Such a year abroad is very expensive and I need your help to make this dream come true. While most of the costs are covered by the Ministry of Education, Families, Senior Citizens, Women and Youth (BMBFSFJ), the total costs for my IJFD in New Zealand are around 12,000 euros. This includes: preparation seminars, supervision, insurance, flights, accommodation, and daily expenses. While most of these costs are covered by the Ministry, there is still a gap of 4.100 € to 4.600 € that I need to fill.

Important:
Donations are tax-deductible as I am working with a registered charity organisation. For that it is important, that you send me your full address, so I can transfer the necesary details to the organisation, who then provides you with a donation receipt.
Please also include the code RRH7Z in the payment reference so that the organisation can connect your donation with my profile.
